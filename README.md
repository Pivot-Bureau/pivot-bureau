## How AI search visibility works

AI engines like ChatGPT, Gemini and Perplexity don't just rank pages — they synthesise answers from structured data, schema.org markup, entity relationships, and citations across the web. We measure your site across eight dimensions:

- 🤖 **AI Search Visibility** — how often AI engines actually mention your brand
- 📍 **GEO** (Generative Engine Optimisation) — geographic + local entity signals
- ❓ **AEO** (Answer Engine Optimisation) — Q&A-formatted content that AI can quote
- 🔗 **Entity SEO** — knowledge-graph identity (Organization, LocalBusiness, sameAs links)
- 🏷️ **Schema.org** — structured data coverage and validity
- 📚 **Semantic SEO** — topical authority and entity disambiguation
- 📝 **Traditional On-Page** — titles, metas, H1s, canonicals, internal linking
- 🌐 **Off-Page Signals** — backlinks, directory citations, social proofs
